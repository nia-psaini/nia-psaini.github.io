Personal Statement

My Goals and Values

 I am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, building on an Associate’s Degree in Liberal Arts: Science, Math, and Technology earned at Madison Area Technical College (MATC). My experience spans technical coursework, customer service roles, and leadership in student organizations.

 Working as a front-end associate at Walmart, I gained skills in handling sensitive financial data and training new employees, which strengthened my attention to detail, communication, and ability to work under pressure. Similarly, my role as a customer service representative at Janak Trucking LLC involved managing confidential billing data and digitizing records, further developing my focus on privacy and organization.

 Alongside work experience, I have taken on leadership roles in clubs such as Ladies of Royalty and Prevention Players, where I organized events and managed budgets. These opportunities enhanced my skills in accountability, teamwork, and long-term planning—qualities essential in cybersecurity.

 I am passionate about using technology to protect sensitive information, especially in healthcare settings, and I am eager to apply my knowledge of programming languages like JavaScript and C/C++ in a collaborative environment. I am committed to continuous learning and contributing effectively to a team focused on cybersecurity and data protection.
